In Paris | Le Marais

Located in the 4th District, the heart of Le Marais, with two beautiful entrances are historically known as "Passage des Singes". Such ideal address accommodates this luxury and fully furnished apartment. Upon entering the building, from either entrance, a merge manicured inner courtyard welcomes you with 5 story elegantly designed wall. The apartment is entered through this private and beautiful inner courtyard providing an abundant natural light through the floor to ceiling double pane windows located in the kitchen and Master bedroom. Additional natural light comes in through the floor-to-ceiling double pane windows located in the living room. All windows have inner wooden shutters, allowing for complete and quiet privacy. The apartment has generous closet space plus a walk-in closet. All appliances, luxury lamps and electrical equipment are of good quality. Wi-Fi, TV, washing machine and dish washer all available. Offered as a turnkey property, furnished down to linens, flatware and ready for immediate occupancy
